Methea guides graduate students from assignment brief to structured findings — building the framework, methodology and instruments with them, never for them.

The brand idea — ink on paper, marker in hand

Every screen feels like a well-kept page in a smart student's favourite book: cream paper, confident serif ink, and the occasional swipe of a highlighter marking what matters. Technology disappears; the craft of research remains.

ii.
Logo

The Swipe

Playfair Display 400, sentence case, tracking −4.5% — letters sit close, like a word written in one breath. One confident diagonal marker stroke through the lower half: the moment a student finds what matters. Stroke is a separate SVG layer (#DDFF55 at 80–90%, multiply blend) so ink always reads through, and it can draw itself in ~400ms on the landing.

iv.
Typography

Three voices, tightly set

Playfair = the scholar (moments of meaning). Schibsted Grotesk = the assistant (controls, never decorates). Source Serif = the reading voice (anything longer than two sentences).

Tracking is part of the brand: display type is set tight — wordmark −4.5%, display −2.5%, headings −1.5%. Words hold together like handwriting, not like a banner. UI and body text stay at default tracking for legibility.

v.
Tone of voice

A brilliant supervisor with time for you

Co-constructive, not interrogative
Always start from what the student brought. Confirm, refine, build on — never quiz from zero.
Precise, not cold
Academic accuracy with human warmth. We say "let's check" not "error detected".
Reasoned, always
Every suggestion carries its why. No oracle answers — show the logic chain.
Calm under deadline
Our user is anxious at 2am. We are the steady voice: one step, then the next.

Microcopy in practice

Don't"Enter your research question."
Do"Based on your brief, your question seems to be about X — does this capture it?"
Don't"Invalid input. Theory selection required."
Do"Pick 2–4 theories to build on — or browse the full library if none of these feel right."
Don't"AI has generated your methodology."
Do"Here's the methodology your framework points to — and why each choice follows."

Layout rules

Single column, max 720px — a page, not a dashboard. Radius 6/10/14px. Spacing in 8px steps. Borders 1px Stone, shadows almost none (paper sits flat). Marker yellow strip on the left edge = Socratic gates only. Every screen answers: what did I bring, what does Methea suggest, why, what's next.

Do / Don't

Do
  • Highlight 3–7 words max, mid-sentence, like a real marker
  • Playfair for meaning, Schibsted for mechanics
  • Lime as glow/stroke (logo, hover) — never as fill for areas
  • Show the why beside every AI output
  • Keep display type tight (−1.5% to −4.5%), UI/body at default
Don't
  • No pure white #FFF backgrounds — always warm paper
  • No marker colours as button fills or full backgrounds
  • No Playfair below 18px, no tight tracking on body text
  • No "AI-generated ✨" sparkle clichés — we are ink, not magic
