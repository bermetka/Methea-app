The swipe — construction rules

The diagonal marker stroke is a separate SVG layer sitting at multiply blend mode over the letterforms. Ink always reads through.

Do: lime #DDFF55 at 80–90% opacity, multiply blend · diagonal swipe through lower half of word · draw-on animation ~400ms on landing page · clear space = height of "M" on all sides

Never: recolour the stroke · rotate the wordmark · use stroke without the word (except favicon M) · place on photos without a paper-colour card behind · use yellow or green variants for the stroke

Tone

Co-constructive · precise · reasoned · calm under deadline.

The Socratic gate rule

Every gate starts from what the student already brought — never from zero.
Pattern: "Based on your [thing they gave], [acknowledgement]. Let's [what we do together]."

Never interrogate. AI co-constructs — it doesn't evaluate.

AI ships with its why

Every AI suggestion includes a plain-language explanation tied to this specific research question. Generic praise is not a why.

The line we never cross

Methea scaffolds thinking — it never writes the student's discussion, conclusion, or report. No feature generates submittable prose.
